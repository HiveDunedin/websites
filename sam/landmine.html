Landmine

watch out for landmines! they are hidden underground,and come out when you're right beside them!landmines make sure you don't run in these areas, or else!

they only have one health and detonate when something(or someone unlucky)goes on them then WHOOM! they blow up and do a tonne of damage.

if you look veeeeeerrrrry closely you may spot 'em before they activate.

tips

  • if you spot them,and another enemy is there(maybe a tank) lure them where the landmine is they will blow them up!(you're welcome)
  • also try place a block over them and they will blow it up and they will drop the block again after blowing up (for your collection!)
