controls

in touchscreen,the controls will be,tap once in the direction you want to go in.tap and drag to use selected item (blocks,weapons,etc.) tap the cube/ball to consume something.(health packs,potions,etc.) if you have auto-consume on, it will automatically consume it if you save it,you will have to do it manually. if you put it in the emergency pack,it will compress it into one emergency pack. use it in emergencies and all the stuff you have compressed in will be consumed, all at the same time.

modes

freedom mode is when enemies are in the game. there are bosses and there is a big space to play on. arena mode is when there is a very limited space,you can toggle enemies on/off and bosses. it is made for multiplayer mini-games. challenge mode is a lot what it sounds like.loads of bosses,it is made for experts.there are a lot of enemies,and cool drops can be common.(you will be able to toggle it off/on)practice is basically the opposite.made for people who find the game hard. you can also toggle stuff like enimies on/off

difficulty

beginner (1) is easiest. infinity(8)is hardest. enemies get stronger and smarter as the difficulty increases.this is a list. 1=beginner 2=apprentice 3=intermediate 4=advanced 5=expert 6=master 7=hardcore and 8+=infinity
